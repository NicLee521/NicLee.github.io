LAW (Legends and Warfare)

About the Project

Legends and Warfare is a project I put a lot of work into during my sophmore and junior year of college. This game is a 3/4 top down competitive MOBA game with towers, abilities, and minions. I worked on quite a few things such as fog of war, MOBA towers, helping with networking player abilities, networking ai MOBA minions, and many other general tasks. I also put time in as the programming lead during one semseter as I wrote on the previous page. Follows are a couple examples of the stuff I worked on throughout the duration of this project.

Code Examples

Moba Towers

For a majority of my time on this project I worked with the towers. Getting them to target the player at the proper times, choose between when to target player and minion, have the top part of the tower follow the target, have the bullet follow the target, and to die properly. All of these features together create what is arguably one of the most important features in a MOBA type game.

Networked Player Abilities

During my time on this project I also worked a lot with the player abilities and making sure they are networked in the game. I mostly worked on the abilities of the defender and the assualt class. These abilities were the defender's sandbag wall, the assualt's landmine ability, and the assualt's fire rate buff.
