About Me

I am very passionate about video games and all things computers. I am a huge sucker for strategy games such as the Total War and Fire Emblem series. I started coding at a relativley late stage in my life during my senior year of highschool but I fell in love with it instantly. Though I may not have started coding as early as I would've liked to, I've always been around computers though as most of my generation has. I've worked in a variety of languages and different engines such as the Unity and Unreal engines. I really enjoy making base game mechanics and I have a couple years experience with networking due to a twin stick shooter moba that a team of other students and I worked on during my sophmore and junior years of college.

Projects

Legends And Warfare

One of my main projects I've been working on since I've started college. It is an entirely networked twin-stick shooter MOBA with 5 different characters, each with 3 different abilities each. It is entirely done by students all around my age and many whom I've now worked with for 2 years and have become great friends with. It has all the elements as any normal MOBA would with towers, minions (or creeps), and a destroyable base(or nexus). It also has the same progression as any other MOBA with the players having to destroy the towers until you make it to the enemies base where you can then destroy their base and win the game.
